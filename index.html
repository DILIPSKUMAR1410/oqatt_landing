Oqatt is a decentralised social media platform which allows user's to speak their true self. It leverages blockchain to empower users and eliminates the central entity which has hidden agendas.

Features

Anonymous

Anonymity removes the impact of user's free speech that might otherwise have on their reputation.

Decetralised

No more data harvasting and jeopardising user privacy. Blockchain provides higher level of trust to user by ensureing their privacy.

Actionable report system

Report hate speech and that will reflect on abuser's Integrity profile.

Integrity profile

Check out user's integrity profile and judge how they expressed themself in previous threads.

Configure thread participation

Ensure minimum number of user particiation or block user's on the basis of their comments or thread topic.

Short lived threads

Threads gets destroyed after some pre configured time adding an additional level of security.
